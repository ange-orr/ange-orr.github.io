Where luxury meets intention. Indulge in customized facial treatments designed to elevate your natural glow. We use only clean, effective, and cruelty-free products chosen with care for your skin’s unique needs. Rooted in honesty and guided by expertise, your skincare experience will be focused solely on your skincare goals— delivered to you in the comfort and privacy of your own home.

Barrier Facial Repair: his restorative facial is great for everyone, especially those with sensitive, inflamed or barrier compromised skin. It’s designed to boost the skin’s microbiome and strengthen the barrier function by incorporating ingredients such as probiotic coconut, cactus pear, hyaluronic acid, peptides and ceramides. Facial massage includes chilled globes for soothing effect.

Rejuvenation Facial Illumination Facial: Brighten and refresh your complexion with this glow-enhancing treatment. Featuring a potent blend of Vitamin C, micro kale, green tea, blue tansy, and bakuchiol—nature’s gentle alternative to retinol—along with hydrating hyaluronic acid for a smooth, luminous finish. A soothing facial massage with chilled globes adds the perfect cooling touch to calm and revitalize the skin. This facial is perfect for those who are looking to tighten and rejuvenate the skin for a lovely glow. It’s packed with naturally aromatic ingredients that firm and soothe the skin, including polyphenol-rich green tea, fruit acids (like papaya, fermented lemon, & berries), banana, turmeric, resveratrol, vitamins, and antioxidants. Facial massage can include Gua sha if you’d like.

Clear Skin Facial: This facial is designed for oily or break out prone skin. It will gently cleanse away impurities, soothe redness and irritation and hydrate the skin. Enjoy the benefits of key ingredients: matcha green tea, peat, kaolin clay, cucumber extract, safflower oil, allantoin, vitamin B5 & B3, hyaluronic acid, and salicylic acid. Facial massage will be customized to meet the unique needs and condition of your skin.
